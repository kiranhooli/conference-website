KLE Society


KLE is a byword in India’s Educational spectrum. Its success story of ten decades portray the dedication with which millions of people worked selflessly in the making of the ‘KLE’. Its 256 institutions spread across Karnataka, Maharashtra, Goa, Delhi etc., narrate amazing stories differently, yet, a harmony is maintained without a miss. Our alumni spread across the world are unofficial ambassadors promoting ‘Brand KLE’.

Our College

Bachelor of Computer Application was started in the year 1999 by the RLS Institute with a mission to provide high quality Computer education to the students. The program aims to bridge the gap between the IT industries and the institutes by imparting in depth knowledge of cutting edge technologies and thus producing the most diverse talents to the students opting BCA. To supplement the knowledge of students and to bring them with the main channel autonomous status was given to our college by NAAC and has started functioning from the academic year 2012-13. KLE’s BCA College aspires to be a premier technical institute to impart quality and innovative technical education with latest technology that will meet the requirements of all its stakeholders.

About the Conference

The objective of this conference is to provide a platform for students, researcher, academicians and industrial people to share and exchange their ideas on recent computational trends in research and application.
